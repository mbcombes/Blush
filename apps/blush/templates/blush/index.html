Services

Our stylists here at Blush offer a variety of services. Listed below are a some of the services that our stylist offer. If you are looking for something that isn't listed, give us a call and one of our professionals will help you!

Cuts

We have professionals to meet all your haircut needs. Bring in the whole family!

Colors

Need a change? Or maybe want to try something crazy? Or did you get a bad color and need some help fixing it? We have a stylist for all your hair coloring needs.

Special Events & Weddings

Need your hair and makeup done for your Wedding? Want to look amazing for your homecoming dance? We can help! Many of our stylists can help to make your day perfect and stress free!

Specialized Treatments

From brow waxing, hair masks, or even a brazilian blowout, our professionals can meet your every need!

Walk-ins are welcome!

We are located in historic down town Sand Springs. Come check out our beautiful downtown shops and restarants and drop in for a visit. We would love to meet your hair and beauty needs that day, or get you scheduled for a time that works best for you!

While you are here you can check out our sister company Rose Anne Adoring Boutique that is located in the front of our salon! They have a ton of cute clothes, jewelry, and season items perfect just for you!
